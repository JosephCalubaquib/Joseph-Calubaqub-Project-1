My School

This school is located in West Haven, CT. The School is very expensive, but luckily they provide scholarships to students. The food on campus is not very good and the school has yet to fix the issue. Like all schools, there are some good professors and some bad professors. Overall this school is okay and a good place to learn.

Fun Stuff

Smash Bros. Ultimate was released on December 7th, 2018. This version of Smash Bros. has included all the previous fighters from the whole series like Snake, Mario, and Wolf. Along with those characters, they included some new characters like Ridley and King K. Rool. The game also has some DLC characters that have yet to be release. The only DLC characters that are known and could be played are Piranha Plant from the Mario Series on January 30th and Joker from the Persona Series on April 17th.
